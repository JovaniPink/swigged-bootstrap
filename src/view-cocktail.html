CLASSIC COCKTAILS

Morimotini

Chef Morimoto's take on a Saketini. Well balanced, with Haku, Suntory's premium Japanese rice vodka and Chef's private label Junmai Sake. Clean, bold, and easy drinking - our pan asian take on a Classic Martini and Saketini.

Ingredients
  • 1 oz Haku Japanese rice-based Vodka
  • 1 oz Morimoto Junmai Sake
  • Cucumber (sliced, garnish)
Procedure

Build drink on ice and stir, served up. - Garnish with cucumber
